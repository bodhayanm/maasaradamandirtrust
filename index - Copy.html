About Us

Spreading the teachings of the Holy Trio

শ্রীশ্রীরামকৃষ্ণ, মা সারদা ও স্বামী বিবেকানন্দের আদর্শে প্রতিষ্ঠিত।

“We, the devotees of Sri Sri Ramakrishna, the Holy Mother Maa Sarada, and ‘Nara-rishi’ Swami Vivekananda, are organised to spread the teachings of the Trio under the banner of the Jagat Janani Maa Sarada Sevashram Trust.”

Founded in 2015, the Trust cares for two Mandirs — at Baganpara, Santiniketan and at Daranda — and carries their spirit into daily life through worship, free education, health care, and the service of the needy as Nara Narayan.

Nara Narayan Seva

Serving the Divine in every person

Since November 2025, the Trust serves free daily meals to ten physically and financially distressed people. Old and new clothes are given to the needy at least twice a year, and new clothes to Vivek Pathsala students at Durga Puja.

Worship, Education & Health

The everyday life of the Sevashram

Puja & Worship

পূজা ও উপাসনা

  • •Daily puja and Sandhya Aarti in both temples.
  • •Tithi Puja — birthdays of Thakur, Maa & Swami Vivekananda.
  • •Guru Purnima celebrated each year.
  • •Jagaddhatri worship introduced from 2025.

Education

বিবেক পাঠশালা

  • •Two free coaching centres — ‘Vivek Pathsala’ at Daranda & Baganpara.
  • •Reading of Kathamrita, Bhagavat & lives of the direct disciples; a Geeta learning class.
  • •Mostly first-generation, SC/ST learners — three tribal students earned places at Visva-Bharati University.

Health & Self-defence

স্বাস্থ্য ও আত্মরক্ষা

  • •Free Homeopathy chamber running in Daranda since 2019.
  • •Free eye camps — with Jayeeta Ganguli Dutta Memorial Trust — have restored clear vision to many.
  • •Self-defence programme introduced at Daranda & Baganpara.
Also at the Sevashram

More of what we offer

Annual Diksha
Initiation twice a year by Swami Satyaprakashananda of Baranagar Ramkrishna Ashram — over 120 devotees initiated.
Probochan & Talks
Discourses on Thakur, Maa and Swamiji by sadhus and invited speakers.
Udbodhan Publications Book Sale
Books and the Udbodhan Patrika made available and distributed at our centres.
Pallimangal Products
Dhoop, ghee, honey, jam and pickle from Kamarpukur, at our counter.
Daily Prasad
Prasad of Maa Sarada available every day, at a minimum cost of ₹80.
COVID-19 Relief
Food packets to the needy, and 30 days of cooked meals for patients at a covid centre.
